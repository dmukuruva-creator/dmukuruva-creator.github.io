// about

I'm an undergraduate at Dartmouth College studying mathematics at the graduate level. My approach to the subject prioritizes motivated exposition — every definition should earn its place, and every theorem should feel inevitable once you understand the machinery behind it.

I'm drawn to the interplay between algebra and topology: how the fundamental group of the punctured plane encodes the behavior of analytic continuation, or how the sign of a determinant captures global orientability. These connections between subfields are, to me, what mathematics is fundamentally about.

I maintain detailed LaTeX notes for every major course — written to make explicit every property invoked and every structural analogy at play.
