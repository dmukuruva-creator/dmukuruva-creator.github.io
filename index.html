About

Mathematics, Engineering, and Discovery

I'm a junior at Dartmouth double-majoring in Mathematics and Biomedical Engineering, taking coursework at the graduate level — including functional analysis, honors probability, and complex variables.

My research spans two domains: pure mathematics (an open problem at the intersection of linear algebra, geometry, and complex analysis, published at JMM 2025) and biomedical science (single-cell RNA sequencing of gynecological cancers at the Goods Lab, Thayer School of Engineering).

Outside the lab I teach as a Physics TA, tutor for Dartmouth Emerging Engineers, and serve as a Residential Advisor. I'm drawn to hard problems and the tools to solve them.
